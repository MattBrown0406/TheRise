Privacy Policy

The Rise is an iOS fly-fishing companion for Central Oregon waters, hatches, fly selection, and catch logging.

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Overview

The Rise is designed to provide fishing condition guidance while keeping personal data collection limited. This policy explains what information the app may use, how it is used, and what third-party services may process information when you use the app.

Information You Provide

  • Catch log entries: You may add fish species, length, water, fly, notes, date, time, conditions, and photos.
  • Saved waters and preferences: You may save favorite waters, choose a home water, enable beginner mode, or mark flies as worked or ignored.
  • Support emails: If you contact support, your email address and message are used to respond to your request.

Location

If you allow location access, The Rise may use your approximate or precise location to rank nearby waters and improve water recommendations while you are planning or moving between fishing areas. Location is used for app features and is not sold.

Photos

If you add a photo to a catch log entry, the app uses that photo for your personal fishing journal. Photos are intended to remain on your device unless a future account sync or sharing feature is added and you choose to use it.

Fishing And Weather Data

The Rise may request public fishing-related information from legitimate external sources, including weather services, river gauge providers, and fishing report sources. These requests help generate water conditions, flow information, hatch context, and fly recommendations.

External services may receive standard technical information from your device or network, such as IP address, device/browser details, request time, and requested URL, according to their own privacy practices.

Subscriptions And Purchases

If you purchase a subscription, payment is processed by Apple through your App Store account. The Rise may use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and unlock paid features. The app does not receive or store your full payment card information.

Local Storage

The Rise may store recent fishing data, saved waters, preferences, fly feedback, and catch log information locally on your device so the app remains useful in areas with limited service.

Data Sharing

The Rise does not sell your personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that help operate app features, such as Apple for subscriptions, RevenueCat for purchase status, and public data providers for fishing conditions.

Children

The Rise is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact support so we can help address it.

Changes

This policy may be updated as The Rise evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
