Fish the right Central Oregon water at the right time.

The Rise helps anglers compare local waters, understand likely hatch windows, choose practical fly patterns, and keep a simple catch journal.

Daily Water Picks

Rank rivers, lakes, and spring creeks by fishability, prime windows, conditions, and local hatch logic.

Bug-To-Fly Matching

Use visual references for aquatic insects and artificial flies so newer anglers can match what they see on the water.

Offline Ready

Save the latest daily water data for early starts and low-service areas, with manual refresh when signal is available.

Built For Central Oregon

Designed around the waters anglers actually talk about before a trip: the Deschutes, Metolius, Crooked, Fall River, Cascade lakes, reservoirs, and smaller nearby options.

  • Fishability ratings on a 1-10 scale
  • Prime fishing windows based on conditions and hatch timing
  • Recommended dries, nymphs, emergers, streamers, terrestrials, and stillwater patterns
  • Saved waters, trip mode, fly-box planning, and catch logging

For Newer And Experienced Anglers

The Rise keeps the daily answer simple while still showing the reasoning behind the recommendation.

  • Beginner-friendly hatch and fly-stage explanations
  • Confidence labels for recommendations
  • Fly feedback so anglers can track what worked and what did not
  • Photo catch log with fish, length, water, fly, and conditions
