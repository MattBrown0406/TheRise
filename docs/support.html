Common Questions

  • Fishing conditions: The Rise is designed to help compare options, not guarantee fishing success. Always use local judgment on the water.
  • Regulations: Anglers are responsible for checking current Oregon fishing regulations before fishing.
  • Offline use: The app is designed to store recent water information locally so it remains useful when service is weak.
  • Subscriptions: If you subscribe through the App Store, subscription management and cancellation are handled through your Apple account settings.

Privacy Notes

The Rise is built to keep fishing planning practical and lightweight. Catch logs and saved waters are intended to stay on the device unless a future synced account feature is added. Location, when enabled, is used to help rank nearby waters and improve trip planning.

If subscription features are enabled, purchase status may be handled by Apple and RevenueCat so the app can unlock paid features.

Safety

River flows, weather, road access, wildfire conditions, ice, and reservoir levels can change quickly. Check official sources and local conditions before heading out.
