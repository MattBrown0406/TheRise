AUTO-RENEWABLE SUBSCRIPTIONS
To locate the subscriptions:
1. Launch The Rise. No account is required.
2. Tap Pro in the bottom navigation.
3. The Pro screen displays The Rise Pro Annual by default.
4. Use the Monthly / Annual toggle to display The Rise Pro Monthly or The Rise Pro Annual.
5. Tap Subscribe Monthly or Subscribe Annual to open Apple’s purchase confirmation.

Before purchase, the Pro screen displays the selected subscription title, duration, localized StoreKit price, auto-renewal and cancellation disclosure, Restore Purchases, Privacy Policy, and Terms of Use.

Products submitted with this version:
• The Rise Pro Monthly — therise_pro_monthly — one month
• The Rise Pro Annual — therise_pro_annual — one year

PHOTO PICKER
To verify the catch-photo flow:
1. Tap Log.
2. Tap New Entry.
3. Tap Choose File in the Photo field.
4. Choose Camera or Photo Library and grant the requested permission.
5. Select a photo, complete the required catch fields, and save the entry.

Build 6 includes NSCameraUsageDescription, NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription, and NSLocationWhenInUseUsageDescription.
